Wood Fired Cheese Pizza

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

Procedure

  1. Mix flour, water, salt and olive oil in large mixer.
  2. Toss the dough like a boss.
  3. Layer with sauce and cheese
  4. Bake in wood fired oven for like 6 minutes
