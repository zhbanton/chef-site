About Jake

This is a personal website for my friend Jake who is also a pastry chef. This is the space where he would write some information about himself. Jake has worked as a pastry chef and bread baker in New York and Boston for over 10 years. He makes bomb pizza and ice cream and loves his girlfriend Maria and his dog Linus. His style involves both creating interesting takes on classic flavor combinations, always with a creative twist and appreciation of the best local ingredients.
